Q9 Chimera is a Drop-in multi-layout PCB for Keychron Q9.
Supports ortholinear layout with central or right-hand numpad, and traditional row-stagger with right hand numpad.
Supports up to two independent rotory encoder positions. 
6u spacebar option supports off-center switch position in either the standard orientation or flipped.
